Lagos

Tafawa Balewa Square (TBS) in Lagos is a historic ceremonial ground located on Lagos Island. Originally known as the “Race Course” in colonial times, it was redeveloped in 1960 to mark Nigeria’s independence and later formally opened in 1972. The square spans about 14.5 hectares and can hold over 50,000 people, making it one of the largest public venues in the city.
